Company FAQ - TechStore Pakistan

Q: What are your business hours?
A: We are open Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 8 PM PKT. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Q: Where are you located?
A: Our main office is in Karachi, Clifton Block 5. We also have branches in Lahore (Gulberg) and Islamabad (F-7 Markaz).

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach us on WhatsApp at +92-300-1234567, by email at support@techstore.pk, or call us at 021-35896543.

Q: What is your return policy?
A: We offer a 7-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original packaging and unused condition. Electronics have a 3-day return window.

Q: Do you offer cash on delivery?
A: Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) across all major cities in Pakistan including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, and Multan.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Same-city delivery takes 1-2 business days. Nationwide delivery takes 3-5 business days via TCS or Leopard Courier.

Q: Do you offer warranties?
A: All electronics come with a minimum 1-year warranty. Laptops and smartphones come with a 2-year warranty. Accessories have a 6-month warranty.

Q: How can I track my order?
A: You will receive a tracking SMS with your courier tracking number once your order is dispatched. You can also log in to your account on our website to check order status.

Q: Do you offer student discounts?
A: Yes! Students with a valid university ID card get 5% off on all laptops and tablets. Visit any of our branches with your student ID.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Cash on Delivery, EasyPaisa, JazzCash, bank transfer, all major debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and HBL Pay.
